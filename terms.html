SMS Notification Program — Faire Punch List Tracker

Program description

This SMS program sends internal job-assignment notifications to Pennsylvania Renaissance Faire crew, staff, and volunteers who are enrolled in the Faire Punch List Tracker. It is not a marketing or promotional messaging program, and is not available to the general public.

Consent

By providing a phone number to a tracker administrator, you consent to receive SMS text messages related to job assignments within the tracker. Consent is not a condition of any purchase, since no purchase is involved in this program. See our SMS Consent Script for the exact language used to collect consent.

Message frequency and costs

Message frequency varies depending on work assigned to you. Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

How to opt out

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. You will receive one final confirmation message. Reply HELP for assistance, or contact your tracker administrator to have your number removed.

No sharing with third parties

We do not share, sell, or transfer mobile phone numbers or opt-in status collected for this program to any third party or affiliate for marketing or promotional purposes.

Supported carriers

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. This program is intended for use on major US mobile carrier networks.
