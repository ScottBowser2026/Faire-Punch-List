SMS Consent Script

Exact language used to collect consent for text notifications — Faire Punch List Tracker

How consent is collected

End users opt in in person or by phone when they are onboarded to a work crew by a Pennsylvania Renaissance Faire administrator. The administrator asks the following, and only enters the person's phone number into the Faire Punch List Tracker if they agree:

"Would you like to receive text message notifications when new jobs are assigned to you in the Faire Punch List Tracker? Message frequency varies — typically a few messages per week, sent only when new work is assigned. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out, or ask your administrator to remove your number."

What happens next

If the person agrees, the administrator enters their phone number into the tracker's Team Management panel. No phone number is ever added without this consent being given first. Phone numbers are never collected through a public website form, QR code, or by the end user texting in.

No sharing with third parties

Consent collected here is used only for this program. Phone numbers are never shared, sold, or transferred to any third party or affiliate for marketing or promotional purposes. See our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions for full details.
