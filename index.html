Help / User Manual

Logging in

Enter your 4-digit PIN. The tracker remembers you after that on the same device, until you log out.

  • Forgot your PIN? Click "Forgot your PIN?" on the login screen, type your name, and a new one gets emailed to you.
  • Want to change it? Click "Change my PIN" at the top of the page once logged in.

What you'll see

By default you only see your own crew's jobs. Click "View all department tasks" to see everything, and again to switch back.

Updating a job's progress

Drag the slider, then attach a photo or a short note and click Save (or press Enter) to actually commit the change. Without a photo or note, Save won't do anything — every update needs some evidence behind it. Notes and photos are saved permanently on the job, visible to anyone who opens it later.

Marking complete / reopening

"Mark complete" jumps a job to 100% and moves it to Completed Projects. Reopen it from that list if it isn't actually finished.

Editing a job

Click "Edit" to change its description, owner, crew, or dates, then Save changes. Reassigning to a different person requires a reason, and doesn't take effect immediately — the new person has to accept it first (see below).

Reassigning a job to someone else

Change "Person responsible" in Edit, type a reason, and save. The job stays with the current owner until the new person accepts it — they'll see Accept/Decline buttons right on that job once it's their turn to review. All existing notes and photos carry over automatically once accepted.

Adding a new job

Use "Add a job": pick a person (or "New person..." for someone not yet in the tracker), fill in the description, start date, and due date — all three are required. No approval needed to create a normal job. Jobs within each crew are sorted by due date automatically, soonest first.

Park and Delete

Managers requesting either one sends it to admin for approval first — you'll get an email once it's approved or denied. Admin's own Park/Delete actions happen immediately after a confirmation prompt. Only admin can create a brand-new job as long-range from the start; managers get there by creating it normally, then requesting Park.

Photos

Click any thumbnail to view it full-size; click again to close. Remove a photo with the small × on its corner, if you have edit access to that job.

Notifications

You'll get an email whenever a job you own changes, whenever a Park/Delete/reassignment request you're involved in gets decided, and if your PIN is ever reset. If you have a phone number on file, you'll also get a text the moment a brand-new job is assigned to you.

Admin: Team Management

  • Add a person: one form for name, email, phone (optional, for texts), temporary PIN, and crew — emails them their PIN automatically.
  • Everyone list: edit anyone's email, phone, PIN, or crews inline. Delete removes them entirely and auto-parks their open jobs so nothing's lost.
  • Rename a person: updates their name across every job, their email, phone, and their PIN in one click.
  • Daily summary email: the address here gets a daily digest and is Cc'd on every job update. "Send now" tests it immediately. Also fires automatically once a day at 6 PM Eastern via a scheduled job, independent of anyone having the tracker open.
  • Force refresh everyone: reloads and signs out every open session — useful after a change, or to clear stale tabs.
  • Test mode: blocks everyone but admin from signing in, with a banner shown to all.

Admin: Approving requests

Park, Delete, and reassignment requests all show Approve/Accept and Deny/Decline buttons right on the job. Approving carries out the action and notifies everyone involved; denying clears the request and notifies the requester.

Admin: Backups

"Download backup" and "Restore backup," near the top of the page, are for emergencies — the tracker already saves everything automatically as you go.

Admin: Recovery PIN

If your admin PIN ever breaks, 861029 always logs in as admin. Change your real PIN right after using it. Keep this number private.
