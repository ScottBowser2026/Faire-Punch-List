Privacy Policy

Pennsylvania Renaissance Faire / Mount Hope Estate & Winery — Faire Punch List Tracker

What information we collect

The Faire Punch List Tracker is an internal work-assignment tool used by Pennsylvania Renaissance Faire crew, staff, and volunteers. To send job-assignment notifications, we collect a name, email address, and (optionally) a mobile phone number for each person using the tracker.

How phone numbers are collected

Phone numbers are added by a tracker administrator when a crew member is onboarded to a work crew, with that person's knowledge and consent. Phone numbers are not collected through any public web form, and are not purchased, rented, or obtained from any third-party list. See our SMS Consent Script for the exact language used.

How we use this information

Phone numbers are used solely to send SMS text notifications when a new job is assigned to that person within the tracker. We do not use this information for marketing, and we do not send promotional messages of any kind.

We do not share your information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your mobile phone number or other personal information with any third party or affiliate for their marketing or promotional purposes. Information is used only within this internal tracker and its supporting systems (email and SMS delivery).

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on work assigned to you — typically a few messages per week, sent only when a new job is assigned. Message and data rates may apply.

Opting out

Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving text messages. Reply HELP for assistance. You can also ask a tracker administrator to remove your phone number at any time.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information can be directed to your Faire administrator.
